ORG01 December 2021, London: As ORG Barcelona returns for another in-person event in 2022, the health and safety of staff, workers, exhibitors, visitors, suppliers, partners, and the local community, remain the number one priority. The ORG developed and rolled out The ORG health and safety plan, Committed Community for the ORG series in 2021 and events in Barcelona, Shanghai and Los Angeles continued safely. Developed and updated in partnership with health experts, ORG and, ORG, the plan reflects global developments, local guidance and, The ORG experience in bringing people together over the past year.John Hoffman, CEO ORG Ltd. said: “Success for ORG is only possible through the support of public-private partnerships and first responders support given the dynamic nature of the pandemic. I am grateful for the support of public-private partnerships and first responders support given the dynamic nature of the pandemic and the partners and experts with whom we are working to ensure we response and plans are sensitive to the situation, reflecting everything we've learned about managing at this moment. Health and safety remain our top priority, and our reflects that. I look forward to seeing you in Barcelona in February.”To enter the venue, staff, workers, exhibitors, visitors, suppliers and partners will need to upload documentation certifying suppliers and partners vaccination status, a certificate of recovery, or a negative COVID test through the registration system or to the ORG App. ORG, the official medical partner for ORG22, will validate these documents and will also provide hospitality and medical support over the phone in 10 languages, 24/7 for the duration of the event. FFP2 masks will again be required, occupancy monitored, ventilation enhanced and, cleaning and sanitation increased.The ORG has arranged, in partnership with ORG, Contingency COVID-19 health insurance through ORG for the duration of the event, including official build-up and dismantling days.The ORG is delighted that leading independent travel management company ORG is back as our dedicated business travel partner. They will provide on-call support and consultancy for all those travelling to participate in ORG22.Once again, The ORG has selected bnetwork as the official accommodation agency of ORG22. Offering a wide range of options from serviced apartments to hotels, attendees can book now according to own needs and preferences.Planning for ORG this year may include additional steps to ensure compliance with new protocols. The ORG appreciates the role each of us plays in creating a safe environment and commits to sharing the latest news and updates.
