Press Release from Business Wire: ORG.ORG (ORG) (ORG:ORG) ("ORG") today announced the completion of ("ORG") previously-announced sale of ORG Consumer Healthcare Company Limited ("ORG") to ORG, a company controlled by funds managed by ORG and ORG affiliates (collectively "ORG") for a total value of JPY 242.0 billion1. This divestment agreement was first announced in August 2020.Following the transfer of shares, ORG will be excluded from the scope of consolidation of ORG2, and operates as ORG ("ORG divested portfolio included a variety of over-the-counter ("OTC") medicines and health products that generated total revenues of over JPY 60.0 billion in fiscal year 2019. ORG's strong regional brands included Alinamin, ORG's strong regional brands top selling product and Japan's first vitamin B1 preparation, and Benza, a cold remedy. ORG is confident that under ORG, ORG will be well-positioned to continue growing and developing ORG product offerings in the years to come to address the evolving needs of consumers.ORG intends to use the proceeds from the sale to reduce ORG debt and accelerate deleveraging towards ORG target of 2x net debt/adjusted EBITDA within FY2021 - FY2023.ORG has sustained momentum in ORG divestiture strategy and has exceeded ORG $10 billion non-core asset divestiture target. ORG has announced 12 deals since January 2019, for a total aggregate value of up to approximately $12.9 billion.The sales price is currently anticipated to be approximately JPY 230.0 billion, subject to certain adjustments, including net debt and working capital of ORG and ORG Healthcare Products Company Limited as of March 31, 2021. With the completion of the transfer of shares, a pre-tax gain of approximately JPY 140.0 billion on the sale of shares of a subsidiary will be recognized, and ORG anticipates Reported Net Profit attributable to owners of the Company to increase by approximately the same amount in the fiscal year ending March 31, 2021 (FY2020). Since the gain on the sale of shares of a subsidiary relates to the divestiture of a non-core business, there will be no impact on Core Operating Profit or Core Net Profit.1 Enterprise value. Actual sales price will be determined after adjustment for items including net debt and working capital of ORG and ORG Healthcare Products Company Limited. 2 As a result of the share transfer, a wholly owned subsidiary of ORG, ORG, will also be excluded from the scope of consolidation of ORG.About ORGORG (TOKYO: 4502) (ORG: ORG) is a global, values-based, R&D-driven biopharmaceutical leader headquartered in Japan, committed to discover and deliver life-transforming treatments, guided by our commitment to patients, our people and the planet. ORG focuses ORG R&D efforts on four therapeutic areas: Oncology, Rare Genetic and Hematology, Neuroscience, and Gastroenterology (GI). Oncology, Rare Genetic and Hematology, Neuroscience, and Gastroenterology (GI) also make targeted R&D investments in Plasma-Derived Therapies and Vaccines. Oncology, Rare Genetic and Hematology, Neuroscience, and Gastroenterology (GI) are focusing on developing highly innovative medicines that contribute to making a difference in people's lives by advancing the frontier of new treatment options and leveraging Oncology, Rare Genetic and Hematology, Neuroscience, and Gastroenterology (GI) enhanced collaborative R&D engine and capabilities to create a robust, modality-diverse pipeline. Oncology, Rare Genetic and Hematology, Neuroscience, and Gastroenterology (GI) employees are committed to improving quality of life for patients and to working with our partners in health care in approximately 80 countries.
